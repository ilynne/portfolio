Why I am a Bernie Sanders Delegate

I am a Washington State Delegate for Bernie Sanders, representing the 34th Legislative District. I am running for National Delegate. This is why.

Welcome!

A long time ago I fired up my text-only web browser for the first time. I followed all the links. There was a menu item labeled "view source." "What is that?" I selected it and the bones of the page I was viewing were revealed to me. "OK, I can do this."

You can still find my first website if you use the wayback machine: don't look. We thought it was pretty cool.

Over the years, I have written about CSS, Flash, SVG and more. I used to keep recipes on my site. It's all still around here on a hard drive.

These days, I am a certified (University of Washington) Ruby on Rails developer.
